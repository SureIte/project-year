Community Housing

The guesthouse provides a unique insight into the daily life of the villagers and gives visitors the opportunity to interact with local people. Most of the villagers have land for farming and growing vegetables as well as raising animals such as buffalo, chickens and pigs.

Our guesthouse is equipped with solar panels to supply electricity and water filters that provide clean water. All rooms are equipped with mattresses, towels, fans and slippers. The bathroom is separate from the house and has running water. Guests may also need to bring a flashlight and hygiene items.

Accommodation costs $ 6 per night for two people. Most tourists want to have dinner with the host, you can also join this dinner and eat everything the host cooks. However, very few landlords can speak English, any special food, please tell the community staff directly to help prepare with the landlord.
